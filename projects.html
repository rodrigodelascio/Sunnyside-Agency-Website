COLIBRI

The exciting task of rebranding COLIBRI, a company specializing in innovative technology solutions. COLIBRI sought a fresh identity that would capture their spirit of agility and transformation. The focal point of the logo was a magnificent blue bird, inspired by its name, which means "hummingbird" in Spanish. Hummingbirds are renowned for their grace, energy, and adaptability—characteristics that perfectly aligned with the brand.

CYBERCODE

A rebranding project for CYBERCODE Programming, a cutting-edge software development company. Our mission was to create a brand identity that would accurately reflect their modern approach to coding and showcase their expertise in the digital realm. We began by immersing ourselves in their world, gaining insights into the industry's trends and CYBERCODE's unique strengths. We employed a contemporary colour palette, representing trust, innovation, and growth.

OCTOPUS

The challenge of rebranding OCTOPUS, a forward-thinking technology supplier. With the goal to embody their innovative spirit and establish them as a contemporary leader in the industry. We envisioned a striking visual representation that would capture the essence of OCTOPUS, with a vibrant colour scheme of bright yellow and purple, symbolizing optimism, creativity, intellect, innovation, sophistication, and transformation, instantly exuding energy and modernity.

HEADPHONES

Capturing the essence and allure of a stunning pair of headphones on a vibrant yellow background to showcase the product in its full glory while creating a visually captivating composition that would draw the viewers' attention. The play of light and shadows added depth and dimension, emphasizing the product's contours and textures. The yellow background exuded vibrancy, energy, and a sense of optimism.

PERFUME

The captivating essence of a perfume housed in a unique silver skull bottle against a backdrop of rich purple and blue hues. An image that seamlessly blends elegance, mystery, and a touch of edginess. The choice of the background was strategic, as the colours represented royalty, sophistication, and a hint of mystique, perfectly complementing the overall theme. A mesmerizing ambiance, enhancing the overall mood and visual impact of the image.

BOOTS

The rugged beauty of a pair of brown boots, hanging effortlessly against a sleek black background, capturing the essence of durability, craftsmanship, and timeless style that these boots exuded. The black background provided a striking contrast, allowing the boots to stand out and become the focal point of the image. The overall composition exuded a sense of strength and resilience, capturing its purpose and evoking a desire in the viewer to embark on new adventures.
